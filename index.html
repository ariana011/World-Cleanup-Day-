About the Event

World Cleanup Day is a global environmental movement that unites millions of volunteers across more than 190 countries to clean up litter and mismanaged waste from streets, rivers, beaches, and forests. Held annually in September, the event raises awareness about pollution and encourages communities to work together for a cleaner, healthier planet.
